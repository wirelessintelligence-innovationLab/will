Lab Space

Hardware Resources

Wireless Intelligence & Innovation Lab (WIIL) is equipped with world-class computing and experimental platforms to support advanced research in wireless communications, AI/ML, and embedded systems.

High-Performance Computing

Powerful desktop and remote compute resources for simulation, ML training and data analysis.

  • Apple iMac (M1 / M2 / M3 / M4) — 1 TB storage, 16–32 GB RAM
  • Windows workstations — Intel Core i7, 16 GB RAM, 512 GB–1 TB SSD
  • Remote GPU node — NVIDIA RTX 3050, 64 GB RAM, up to 1 TB SSD (Remote Desktop accessible)

Wireless & Measurement

Industry-grade RF and measurement equipment for software-defined radio and signal analysis.

  • USRP (NI Instruments) — Software Defined Radio platforms
  • Tektronix Digital Storage Oscilloscope (DSO)
  • High-speed Wi-Fi (5 GHz) with up to 1 Gbps throughput

Embedded & Edge Devices

Edge prototypes and microcontroller boards for IoT, V2X and real-time experimentation.

  • Raspberry Pi 5
  • Arduino family
  • Intel Galileo
  • Hardware development kits & sensor modules

Supporting Infrastructure

Peripherals and lab conveniences that support research workflows.

  • Brother LaserJet Printer (high quality)
  • Local and network storage for datasets and experiments
  • Collaboration-ready benches and prototyping stations
